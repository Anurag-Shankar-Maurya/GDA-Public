    {% trans "About the Association" %}

    {% trans "The Global Devotion Association (GDA Taiwan) was founded in 2017 by Qingyu Chen, who graduated from the University of Southern California in 2025. It has been committed to cultural conservation and hopes to provide volunteer opportunities for Taiwanese students to carry out meaningful actions - giving back to society and cultural experiences through exchanges and helping minority groups." %}

    {% trans "Motivation and Mission" %}

    {% trans "We encourage young volunteers to experience and participate in different cultures and lifestyles, and to understand Taiwan's history and differences, hoping to cultivate students' interest and appreciation for Taiwan's diversity and uniqueness. We connect students with volunteers from different cultural and social communities, and establish long-term relationships in various parts of Taiwan, allowing students to give back to the community while learning. We introduce and promote Taiwan's diverse indigenous groups to the world, hoping to preserve these precious cultures through volunteer services and cultural exchange activities by young volunteers." %}

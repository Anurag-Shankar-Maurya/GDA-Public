    {% trans "Real stories of challenge, rejection, and growth" %}

    {% trans "Lin Zhankai" %}

    {% trans "Personal Growth" %} {% trans "Communication" %}

    {% trans "Before the event started, I naively thought that I only needed to prepare the materials well to complete this easy task. However, when it actually began, the harsh reality completely changed my foolish thinking. No matter how well prepared I was, I still felt nervous when talking to strangers." %}

    {% trans "Zheng Fuxin" %}

    {% trans "Courage" %} {% trans "Experience" %}

    {% trans "Through this activity, I learned a lot of valuable experience. I thought that I just needed to practice what to say before the activity, and I was very confident at first. But when the activity started, I saw passers-by walking by one by one, but I didn't take any action. When I mustered up my courage, most passers-by rejected me again and again." %}

    {% trans "Yang Yuquan" %}

    {% trans "Resilience" %} {% trans "Self-Awareness" %}

    {% trans "Before I joined this event, I thought the task was easy, but when I actually participated, I found it very challenging. Being rejected or looked at strangely was really frustrating. However, through this process, I gradually developed the courage to face setbacks and also realized how inadequate my own abilities were." %}

    {% trans "Wang Guanda" %}

    {% trans "Environment" %} {% trans "Teamwork" %}

    {% trans "Through this volunteer activity, I have experienced many things I have never experienced before. Most people rejected us, and some asked if I really wanted to do this volunteer work. The feeling of being rejected is very frustrating, but if we don't stand up and do this today, the environmental protection problems will only get worse." %}

    {% trans "Lian Weixuan" %}

    {% trans "Impact" %} {% trans "Gratitude" %}

    {% trans "This is a great activity that gives us the opportunity to train our speech and convey our ideas to others, and also do our part for the blue planet. Even though we may be just a drop of water, I hope we can stir up more ripples to join us, and eventually the calm water surface will be full of responsive ripples." %}

    {% trans "Cui Xiangyu" %}

    {% trans "Understanding" %} {% trans "Service" %}

    {% trans "This volunteer activity taught me a lot. We thought that publicity was very simple, but we were constantly rejected. The four words 'volunteer service' have become a tool for children to earn hours in the minds of the public. This made me understand one of the original intentions of Global Devotion Volunteer. Protecting the environment starts with you and me!" %}
